Welcome to EMC!

This site is a Japanese tutorial for living on the EarthMC Minecraft server.

EMC uses "Gold" as its currency, written as "G."

Please start by reading "What to do first."

ğŸ’° How to Collect Gold

â‘  Digging

This is the most basic way to make money. Dig for gold outside your territory (in the wilderness). However, due to the risk of player kills, we recommend using an invisibility potion.

â›ï¸ How to Dig for Gold (Details)

Things You'll Need:

  • Efficiency V, Durability III Pickaxe and Shovel
  • Food
  • Potion of Invisibility (for safety)
  • Haste II Potion

How to Dig:

  1. Go outside your territory (in the wilderness).
  2. Drink a Potion of Invisibility (to prevent PK).
  3. Dig down to the recommended depth (around y12 or y22).
  4. Use a mining potion to dig efficiently (e.g., 5 blocks wide x 2 blocks high).

â‘¡ Business (Shop Management)

Earn money by opening your own shop or selling items to buy-back shops like Mali or Yamato.

â‘¢ PK (Player Kill)

This method involves killing other players and stealing their items. It requires PVP skill and is extremely dangerous.

Extra Edition

  • Raid (Ruin Rover): If no one logs in to a town for 42 days, it becomes a ruin (protection is removed) and you can take as many items as you want.
  • Gambling: This is a way to earn money at the casino, but there is a risk of bankruptcy.
  • Company: You can also earn a salary by working for a landscaping company or similar.

ğŸª How to Use the Shop

How to Read the Sign

  1. Name of Seller (If sold out, the name will be red)
  2. Selling or Buying
  3. Item
  4. Price (Example: 64 stones for 1G)

âœ… How to Buy Items

  1. Left-click the sign (or chest) of the shop you want to buy from.
  2. Information ("Shop Information") will appear in the chat box.
  3. Enter the number you want to purchase in the chat box (e.g., 1).
  4. The purchase was successful when "Successfully purchased" appears.

ğŸ”µ How to Create a Shop

  1. Speak to the mayor and have them designate your land as a SHOP chunk.
  2. Place a chest, hold the item you want to sell, and left-click the chest.
  3. The message "Enter in chat, how much..." will appear in the chat.
  4. Enter the selling price in the chat field.
  5. Place the item in the chest to begin selling.

ğŸš« Server Rules

Please follow the rules to ensure a pleasant experience on our server.

  • Cheating: Cheat clients, macros, and AFK auto-kick avoidance are prohibited.
  • Accounts: Alternate accounts (sub-accounts) are not permitted.
  • Chat: Spamming, bullying, hate speech, and sharing personal information are prohibited.
  • Global Chat: /global is English only. Please use Japanese chat /Japanese.
  • RMT: Selling in-game items or services for real money is prohibited.
  • Trolling: Destroying other people's claims (land) or their surroundings is prohibited.
  • Terrain: Significant alteration of natural terrain (large-scale terraforming) is prohibited.

ğŸ˜¼ Hazards and Precautions

EMC has many dangers. Protect yourself.

Danger 1: Item Loss

When you die, your items will be dropped and potentially picked up by others.

Countermeasures: Store your valuables in an ender chest. Only you can see the contents.

Danger 2: Player Penalty in the Wilderness

Unowned land (the wilderness) allows PVP (attacks). Players' locations are visible on the map, making them easy targets.

Countermeasures:

  • Do not venture into the wilderness (use /n spawn to travel).
  • Do not carry valuables when ventured into the wilderness.
  • Drink an invisibility potion (to become invisible on the map).

Danger 3: Scams and Traps in Safe Zones

You may be lured into the wilderness by chat messages (tells) such as "Come here," or you may be killed in a PVP area.

Countermeasures: Do not be persuaded by sweet talk from unknown players or foreigners.
